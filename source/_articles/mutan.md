---
title: Mutan
description: Mutan was a smart contract language that was created by Jeff Wilcke in the early part of 2014.  It was originally called HLL (high level language).  As Solidity matured, Mutan was deprecated.
author: Jeff Wilcke
date: 2014-03-23
---


Jeff Wilcke made commits to [obscuren/mutan](https://github.com/obscuren/mutan) between [23rd March 2014 and 7th July 2015](https://github.com/obscuren/mutan/commits?author=obscuren).

The deprecation was just weeks before the mainnet Frontier launch.
