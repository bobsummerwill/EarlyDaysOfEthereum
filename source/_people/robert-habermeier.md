Robert Habermeier was one of the key early researchers around [Polkadot](https://polkadot.network/) and [Substrate](https://www.substrate.io/). In Gavin Wood's 2018 [Epicenter interview](/videos/gavin-wood-substrate-polkadot-on-chain-governance/), Gavin credited the [Web3 Foundation](https://web3.foundation/)'s research team, "particularly [Rob](/people/robert-habermeier/) at [Parity](/articles/parity-technologies/)," with work on the GRANDPA/SHAFT finality algorithm that was being merged into Substrate and discussed as part of Polkadot's consensus stack.
