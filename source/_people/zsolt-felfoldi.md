Zsolt has been working on Ethereum since 2014, with an initial focus on the Light Client sub-protocol, but also on Swarm and the Geth client in general.

He cofounded Ethersphere in Budapest in 2015 with Viktor Trón, and Daniel Nagy as an umbrella for their development activities, primarily on Swarm.
