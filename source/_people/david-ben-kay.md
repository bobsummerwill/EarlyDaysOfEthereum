David Ben Kay is a lawyer who served as an advisor and legal counsel to the Ethereum Foundation during 2016. Based in Asia, he brought expertise in corporate law and governance to the Foundation during a critical period that included The DAO crisis and the subsequent hard fork decision.

Kay was involved in the legal structuring and governance discussions around the Ethereum Foundation during its early organizational phase, helping navigate the complex regulatory landscape facing the nascent blockchain project.
