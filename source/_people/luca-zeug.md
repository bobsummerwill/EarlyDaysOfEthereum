Luca Zeug, known by the GitHub handle "luclu", was a member of the Mist browser team at the Ethereum Foundation from April to July 2016. Mist was the official Ethereum browser and wallet application, led by Alex van de Sande and developed alongside Jeffrey Wilcke's Go team.
