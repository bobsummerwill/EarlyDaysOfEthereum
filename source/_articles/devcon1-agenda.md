DEVCON1 was held November 9–13, 2015, at Gibson Hall in the City of London. It featured over 50 talks across 5 days, with approximately 400 attendees. The first half of the week focused on research and ÐApp development, the second half on industry and social implications.

The full agenda was preserved via the [Wayback Machine](https://web.archive.org/web/20160107084411/https://devcon.ethereum.org/).
