Meher Roy co-hosted the 2018 [Epicenter interview with Gavin Wood](/videos/gavin-wood-substrate-polkadot-on-chain-governance/). In the introduction, he said that building validator infrastructure through [Chorus One](https://chorus.one/) had made him a better interviewer by giving him a deeper understanding of how proof-of-stake systems work internally, and he steered much of the conversation toward Gavin's design philosophy, [Substrate](https://www.substrate.io/), and governance.
