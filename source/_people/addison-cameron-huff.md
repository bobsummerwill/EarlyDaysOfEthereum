Addison Cameron-Huff was one of Ethereum's earliest lawyers during the project's Toronto phase.

## Ethereum Canada

Episode 14 adds Anthony Di Iorio's first-hand account of Huff's role. When Di Iorio created [Ethereum Canada Inc.](/articles/ethereum-canada-inc/) as a temporary Toronto legal vehicle in early 2014, he says he hired Addison Cameron-Huff as part of the effort to get the project organized and able to hire people.

Di Iorio refers to Huff as his lawyer at the time and says he likes to think of him as "the first Ethereum lawyer we had."
