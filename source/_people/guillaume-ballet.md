[Whisper](/articles/whisper/) was the decentralized messaging layer of the original [Holy Trinity](/articles/holy-trinity/) of Web3, alongside [Ethereum](/articles/stiftung-ethereum/) (computation) and [Swarm](/articles/swarm/) (storage). The protocol was eventually deprecated, with [Waku](https://waku.org/) serving as its spiritual successor.
