Jutta Steiner joined the Ethereum project in November 2014 as Head of Security at ETHDEV. She was announced as a new hire in Gavin Wood's third development update, as the team was building out the Berlin hub ahead of DEVCON-0.

After leaving the Ethereum Foundation in 2015, Jutta co-founded Parity Technologies with Gavin Wood, where she served as CEO until December 2021.
