DEVCON3 was the third public Ethereum developer conference, held at the Moon Palace Resort in Cancún, Mexico from November 1 to 4, 2017. It was a significant scaling up from previous editions, attracting around 2,000 attendees.

## Key Themes

DEVCON3 took place during a period of explosive growth for Ethereum and the broader cryptocurrency ecosystem. The conference covered topics including:

- Scaling solutions (Plasma, sharding, state channels)
- Proof of Stake and Casper research
- Developer tooling and frameworks
- Enterprise Ethereum applications
- Privacy and security
- Decentralized governance

## Notable Presentations

The conference featured talks from many key Ethereum figures including Vitalik Buterin, Vlad Zamfir, Karl Floersch, and others working on Ethereum's core research and development.
